About us

Shop.com - a young and ambitious online store of footwear, clothing and accessories made in Germany. Our mission is to popularize high-quality German goods and prove that wearing German is not only fashionable, but also very profitable. Our main advantage is that we have direct contacts with German manufacturers, and therefore we have the most honest actual prices.

German designers always try to follow fashion trends, which helps our customers to be in a trend. We do not try to chase profit, but we work for our customers, for their maximum comfort and convenience. We want our customers to look stylish for a reasonable price.

Convenient navigation of our site will help you quickly find the thing you need, and managers will help with the choice. Buying at shop.com is the best choice for you.
